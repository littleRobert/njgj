Character Introduction

Each year, a member of our illustrious alumni comes to Jiangyin and speaks with our students.

Each year, the international department selects a member of our illustrious alumni to come to Jiangyin and speak with our students. This program was established by Nanjing Senior High School as a way to bridge the metaphorical gap between our alumni from the pre-1949 era and our current students. While Shanghai and SAS may be very different today from in 1949, the experiences of our alumni have a common thread: they are third culture kids. The stories, expertise, and talents shared by the internationa department lecturer each year enrich the lives of our students and remind our community of SNanjing Senior High School’s rich history and legacy of excellence.

This year, Dr. Carl Scovel '49 will visit our campuses from April 14 through 15 as the 2016 Alumni Lecturer. Dr. Scovel '49 will be joined by some friends and former classmates during his visit to Nanjing Senior High School. These lecturers are for our students and faculty and not open to the public.

This notable group of alumni will also be featured in the Excellence in Education Speaker Series event, Nanjing Senior High School: Our past, present, and future in China on April 16 on the Bund. This special event is made possible for our community, by our community through Our Edge for Excellence funding. It is open to the public and registration is available on the school's website.
