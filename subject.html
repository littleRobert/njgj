Subject Teachers

The students are taught by senior outstanding teachers. They have the compassion, the sense of responsibility and can get along harmoniously with students. They have good communication skills. They are open-minded and can accept new ideas. Foreign teachers mainly comes from the United States, Canada, Britain, Australia and other countries.
